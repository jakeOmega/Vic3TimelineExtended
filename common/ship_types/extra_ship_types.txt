# =============================================================================
# Vic3TimelineExtended — modern (post-vanilla) ship types
# =============================================================================
# These ships extend vanilla 1.13's ship roster into the mod's late-era timeline.
# Each ship is loosely modeled on a vanilla parent type and reuses that parent's
# modification slots (ship_mod_<parent>_*) so we don't duplicate every armor /
# guns / propulsion / range tier.
#
# Era / tech mapping (covers all 4 roles every era 6-12 with 2-3 era overlap):
#   era 6  bombing_aircraft             → fleet_carrier                     (capital)
#   era 6  combined_arms                → amphibious_assault_ship           (troop, retires at PGM)
#   era 6  sonar                        → asw_destroyer                     (escort)
#   era 7  advanced_submarine_technology → nuclear_submarine                (raider)
#   era 7  guided_missiles              → guided_missile_ship               (escort)
#   era 7  nuclear_energy               → nuclear_supercarrier              (capital, extends through era 9)
#   era 8  precision_guided_munitions   → marine_assault_ship               (troop, Cold-War-2 LHA)
#   era 9  network_centric_warfare      → arsenal_ship                      (capital, missile-bus)
#   era 9  network_centric_warfare      → stealth_battlecruiser             (capital, stealth)
#   era 9  network_centric_warfare      → networked_air_defense_cruiser     (escort)
#   era 9  network_centric_warfare      → expeditionary_fast_transport      (troop, fast-doctrine)
#   era 9  rapid_deployment_forces      → stealth_attack_submarine          (raider)
#   era 9  rapid_deployment_forces      → expeditionary_sea_base            (troop, capacity-doctrine)
#   era 10 hypersonic_weapons           → hypersonic_missile_platform       (capital)
#   era 11 directed_energy_weapons      → pulsed_laser_escort               (escort)
#   era 11 swarm_technology             → swarm_coordination_vessel         (capital/support)
#   era 11 swarm_technology             → autonomous_swarm_submarine        (raider, terminal)
#   era 12 antimatter_production        → antimatter_battleship             (capital, terminal)
#   era 12 orbital_weapon_platforms     → orbital_support_amphibious_mothership (troop, terminal)
# =============================================================================
