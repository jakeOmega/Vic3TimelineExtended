# because at most one country fills each role per play. participant_* is APPLIED PER
# COMMITTED COUNTRY in the play and stacks additively — a play with N participants
# gets N× the participant contribution. Reserve participant_* for narrow single-country
# uses (one-off event modifiers, unique character traits); never attach to laws/techs/
# principles or any modifier source many countries hold simultaneously, or escalation
# explodes for plays with many backers (e.g. Britain + Indian princely states).
